HOW PONTUS WORKS

  • Step 1 Register on pontus-slackbot.herokuapp.com
  • Step 2 Type @pontus in the your slack channel or Dm
  • Step 3 Type @pontus save- add your message
  • Step 4 Your messeage will be saved inside the pontus external driver
  • Step 5 Login into your pontus account to see all your saved messages and information from slack
